The sprint does give a focus, and something to challenge suggested work (how does this new thing contribute to the sprint goal?). My concern is removing the sprint would lose the focus, and the flip side is the focus would shift to the overall value delivery rather than a (sometimes) arbitrary short term goal. My current thinking is to introduce regular reviews against the release goal and to gradually fade out the focus on the sprint goal.
