Weak note S99E99

I have been rubbish at writing weeknotes, and have not found time to read the Medium recommended posts, and have also found the recommendations to be not great.

I have found weeknotes to be a good way to stop and think about things during the week, but I am not sure of the value to others. Being totally selfish I prefer to spend the time and money on myself and my family.

So, I have decided to cancel my Medium subscription for now, and see if I miss it.

The End.
