Time flies like a banana

This week feels like time is flying by in a series of curves. Some hours go whizzing by, and some hours drag by. I have had a lot of context switches this week which have been necessary but meant I have felt less productive.

Things from this week

ReactJS and REST calls: more investigation into how to use ReactJS to make async REST calls. It has been a good fun technical challenge, and something I want to continue to tinker with.

Rouvy: I got the turbo trainer wired up to the laptop with an additional ant+ dongle. Zwift was OK but the graphics were a bit clunky and the app exits whenever you stop a ride. I saw (by chance) someone saying they would have a go at Rouvy, so I took a look as well. It has good graphics — a blend of real-world video and computer-drawn avatars, and the same sort of online interaction and metrics that Zwift does. I had a good session on Wednesday across the Nevada desert, and the competitive bit of me wanted to chase down the person I could see about 1/2 mile ahead (I got within 400 metres at one point, then they pulled away again). It has spiked my interest which is good.

Tour de Gwent: I finally got around to registering for the Tour de Gwent in April, so need to get training outside and on the turbo.

Azure DevOps is good, and improving all the time. The docs suggest an online visual pipeline editor but I couldn’t find it.

Things for next week

Tidying up and work and home.

South Wales Agile group talk on “Site Reliability Engineering” on Thursday.

Final day at Companies House on Friday.
