Story Mapping

We had a great session last week talking through a large piece of upcoming work with the team. On the surface it looks like a fairly simple thing to do, but once you dig into more of the details and user journeys it blossoms. We are planning a user story mapping workshop, and to pre-empt it to get my thoughts in order I had a go with CardBoard. It is a fabulous tool and something that was easy to get started with.

Some of the team hadn’t heard about Story Mapping, so I am running a session next week so they are aware of the ocncepts before the workshop. The “Introduction to user story mapping” page was very useful for a simple overview.

A spin off from the “Next Generation of software developers” article was how crap the phrase “Corporate Social Responsibility” is.
