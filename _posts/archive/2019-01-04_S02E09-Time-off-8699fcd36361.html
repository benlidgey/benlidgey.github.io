S02E09 Time off

This week I have been on holiday, and spent loads of quality time with my family. Some great places we went to:

Wittenham Clumps

An Iron Age fort in south Oxfordshire with wonderful views. Saw some people flying remote controlled gliders too which was very impressive. I would have crashed them within 5 minutes.

Forest Farm

A lovely area to walk that is close to town, but hidden away. Has the remains of the Glamorganshire Canal. Shame historical landmarks like that have been lost.

Cwmcarn

An old haunt of mine when I used to go mountain biking. This time we went for a family walk on the Twmbarlwm mountain trail. The weather was clear and cold, but we had a cuppa and flapjack at the trig point at the top in true Brit style. Even the dog made it to the top and back again.

Must be something about Iron Age forts this week.

Next week is back to work and normal routine :-(
